Cheng Command

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: Cheng Command stores your family's data privately in your own iCloud account. We don't collect it, we don't see it, and we never sell it.

What Data the App Collects

Cheng Command stores the following data to provide its features:

  • Family member names and profile colors
  • Calendar events, to-do lists, and notes
  • Meal plans, recipes, and pantry inventory
  • Grocery lists and purchase history
  • Photos attached to recipes
  • iCloud account identifiers used to attribute content to family members

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively in your family's private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This means:

  • Your data lives in your iCloud account — not on our servers
  • Data syncs between your family's devices via Apple's encrypted iCloud service
  • Only family members you invite can access your family's data
  • Apple's privacy and security standards govern how the data is stored and transmitted

Data We Do Not Collect

The developers of Cheng Command have no access to your family's data. We do not:

  • Collect, store, or process your data on any external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Sell or share your data with any third parties
  • Display advertising of any kind

Third-Party Services

Cheng Command uses the following third-party service for one optional feature:

  • Open Food Facts API — used when scanning product barcodes to look up product names. Barcode numbers may be sent to Open Food Facts servers solely to retrieve product information. No personal data is transmitted. Open Food Facts is a free, open-source food database. Their privacy policy is available at world.openfoodfacts.org.

Notifications

With your permission, Cheng Command sends push notifications for events, to-do assignments, grocery list updates, and pantry reminders. These notifications are delivered via Apple's Push Notification service (APNs). You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.

Children's Privacy

Cheng Command is a family app intended for use by all family members including children, under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children independently — all data is managed within the family's shared iCloud account under the control of the account holder.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Delete individual items within the app at any time
  • Delete the app from your device to remove local data
  • To remove all CloudKit data, go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and delete the Cheng Command data

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@chengcommand.app or through the App Store listing.
